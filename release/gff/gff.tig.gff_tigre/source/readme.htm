GFF Tigre Lexical Model

This is a Tigre (ትግሬ / ትግረ, ISO-639-3 tig) lexical model developed to support predictive text in the corresponding GFF Tigre keyboard. The word list has not been reviewed to validate spelling. Spelling refinements and the incorporation of additional word lists are goals of future development of this lexical model.

The corpus is derived primarily from the Eritrea Haddas archives.

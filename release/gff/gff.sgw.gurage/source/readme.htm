Gurage Lexical Model

This is a Gurage (ጉራጌ , ISO-639-2 sgw) lexical model developed to support predictive text the corresponding GFF Gurage Keyboard. The lexical model comprises word frequency lists from the works of Wolf Leslau ("Ethiopians Speak vol. 2 Chaha"), Shahle Jingo (Gurage Fables) and the Sebat Bet Bible of the Ethiopian Bible Society. The respective texts have been normalized under the 2018 experimental orthography of Dr. Fekede Menuta and require the "Zebidar" font to view non-standard characters introduced for the Gurage language. Frequency counts were generated with the "PrimerPrep" application.
